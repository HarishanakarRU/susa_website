Statistics Graduate-Undergraduate Program (SGUP)

SUSA is partnering with the Statistics Graduate Student Association (SGSA) to provide exciting opportunities to the undergraduates in the statistics community!

Stay tuned for updates on by joining our Mailing List, Facebook page , or Discord server

Overview

In Fall 2020, we offered two ways to get involved in our program: mentorship and office hours.

Mentorship

Undergraduates had the chance to be paired up with a graduate student mentor to form a personal and professional relationship. Paired mentees will meet with their mentors monthly to discuss their goals and gain insights into graduate school and career related matters.

Office Hours

Our graduate students hosted weekly office hours to answer any questions you may have about graduate school in statistics or anything else that you would want to talk about. Get to know various graduate students other than your GSI's through these office hours and use them to your advantage to get more perspectives on your plan in life!

The office hours happened on:

  1. October 23, Friday - 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM
  2. October 28, Wednesday - 12:30 PM - 1:30 PM
  3. November 11, Wednesday - 12:30 PM - 1:30 PM
  4. November 20, Friday - 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM
  5. December 4, Friday - 12:30 PM - 1:30 PM

These office hours were hosted on Zoom.
