Statistics Graduate-Undergraduate Program (SGUP)

SUSA is partnering with the Statistics Graduate Student Association (SGSA) to provide exciting opportunities to the undergraduates in the statistics community!

Overview

We will be offering two ways to get involved in our program: a mentorship and office hours.

Mentorship

You will have the chance to be paired up with a graduate student mentor and gain a personal bond with them! Feel free to ask questions from them and you two can have meetings (or check-ins) to discuss your goals or gain opinions on other graduate school related matters.

Apply here: *insert link here* to be matched with a grad student!

*Due to a limited pool of graduate students participating in this mentorship, we will not be able to match every undergraduate student who applies. If you do not make it into this mentorship, don't worry! You did not do anything wrong, and you will still have the chance to connect with graduate students through our office hours

Office Hours

Our graduate students will be hosting office hours weekly to answer any questions you may have about graduate school in statistics or anything that you would want to talk about. Get to know various graduate students other than your GSI's through these office hours and use them to your advantage to get more perspectives on your plan in life!
